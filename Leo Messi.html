2. Barcelona’s Academy (La Masia):
  • Joined: 2000 at the age of 13>
  • Messi moved to Spain to join FC Barcelona's famed youth academy, La Masia, at just 13 years old. His family moved with him to support his career, and Messi’s potential was immediately clear.
  • Due to his small stature (he was diagnosed with growth hormone deficiency), Messi was initially not growing at the same rate as his peers. Barcelona agreed to cover his medical treatment, which allowed him to continue his development.
